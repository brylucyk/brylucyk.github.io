About

Hi, I'm Bry.

As a full-stack developer, I aim to provide smooth functionality with attractive, custom, responsive design.

In my current role of Developer with St. Joseph's Healthcare London, I am helping to build a web-based clinical and research platform that facilitates the collection, interpretation and visualization of patient data. The platform is currently being customized to the needs of the Research 2 Practice and Aging Brain and Memory Clinic at Parkwood Institute, with the ultimate aim of supporting other clinical teams and research groups in the future.

Other projects I'm working on in this role include:

  • building a website that will facilitate the free exchange of assistive devices and medical equipment across Ontario, and;
  • providing project coordination and development support for a mobile app that helps people with concussions structure their daily activities in a way that best promotes recovery.

In my past life, I was Research Coordinator with Lawson Health Research Institute, managing projects related to mental health, homelessness, and smart technology.
